Customer's phone won't scan at the entrance or exit

Most phones are set to automatically dim the screen in low light conditions.  This makes it easy on your eyes and saves battery life but it's hard to read reflective surfaces with dim screens.  If the scanner can't read the QR code, ask the member to please turn up the brightness.

Back to menu

Android Phones

Swipe down from the top of the screen to reveal the Notification Shade. Depending on which Android phone they have, they may have to swipe twice.

Tap and hold the brightness slider.

Drag the brightness slider to the right as far as it will go.

Release the slider.

Back to menu
Apple iPhones

Go to the home screen by pressing the home button

Swipe up from the bottom of the screen.

Toggle Auto-Brightness to Off.

Move the slider as far to the right as you can.

Member's mobile card or reservation won't scan at the entrance or exit

Reservations may not scan for several reasons.

Back to menu

Entrance

No message and no green or red light

This is normally an issue with mobile devices.  The screen is too dim or the customer isn't holding the code close enough to the scanner.

"Card already present" message

The QR code is the same as a member's card number.  This often happens when a family member is parking and the member is already on the lot.  Confirm that there is already a car on the lot, pull a ticket and instruct them to show both the reservation and the ticket when they exit.

If they don't have a family member on the lot, get their card number pull a ticket and instruct them to show both the reservation and the ticket when they exit.  Inform the manager on duty that there is a card that didn't exit and provide the card number.

Exit

"Card not valid for payment" message

This happens at the express lane when the member didn't see the light turn green.  The card was already read and the member can proceed with redemptions or credit card payment.

No message and no green or red light

This is normally an issue with mobile devices.  The screen is too dim or the customer isn't holding the code close enough to the scanner.

"Missing Entry Data" message

The reservation wasn't used at the entrance or another family member used the card to exit instead of the ticket they pulled.  Ask the customer what they used to enter.  (White paper ticket, Plastic card, Reservation)
